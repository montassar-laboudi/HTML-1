About Starbucks

Starbucks Corporation is an American multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery founded in 1971 reserves headquartered in Seattle, Washington. It is the world's largest coffeehouse chain. As of November 2021, the company had 33,833 stores in 80 countries, 15,444 of which were located in the United States

Coffee Trivia

What is the origin of coffee?

Response: Coffee originated from Ethiopia, where it was discovered in the 9th century. It spread to the Arabian Peninsula and later to Europe and the rest of the world.

How should coffee be stored to maintain its freshness?

Response: To maintain the freshness of coffee, it should be stored in an airtight container away from light, moisture, heat, and strong odors. It is best to grind coffee beans just before brewing and avoid storing ground coffee for extended periods as it tends to lose its flavor quickly.

What is the difference between a cappuccino and a latte?

Response: A cappuccino and a latte are both espresso-based drinks, but they differ in their ratio of espresso, steamed milk, and foam. A cappuccino typically has equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and foam, while a latte has more steamed milk and a thin layer of foam on top.
